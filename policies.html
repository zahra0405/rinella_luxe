Business Policies ✦

RINELLA LUXE establishes clear business policies to ensure transparency, consistency, and a positive customer experience throughout the purchasing process.

Shipping Policy

RINELLA LUXE is committed to ensuring that all orders are processed and delivered efficiently. Orders are processed within 1–2 working days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next working day.

Customers will receive an order confirmation email immediately after a successful purchase. Once the order has been shipped, a tracking number will be provided via email to enable customers to monitor the delivery status.

Delivery within Malaysia, including Sabah and Sarawak, typically takes 3–5 working days. Delivery times may vary depending on location, courier schedules, weather conditions, public holidays, or unforeseen circumstances.

A standard shipping fee of RM8 applies to all orders. Customers who spend RM150 and above in a single transaction are eligible for free shipping.

Customers are responsible for ensuring that all shipping information is accurate. RINELLA LUXE will not be held responsible for delays, failed deliveries, or additional charges caused by incorrect information.

Return & Refund Policy

Customer satisfaction is one of the key priorities of RINELLA LUXE. Customers may submit a return or exchange request within seven (7) days of receiving the product.

To qualify for a return or exchange, products must remain unused, unwashed, unworn, free from stains or odours, and in their original packaging with all tags and labels attached.

Returns and exchanges are accepted if:

  • The customer received an incorrect product.
  • The product received is defective or damaged.
  • The product does not match the order confirmation.

Returns and exchanges are not accepted for:

  • Products that have been used, washed, altered, or damaged by the customer.
  • Sale, clearance, promotional, or discounted items.
  • Products returned without original packaging or tags.

Approved refunds will be processed through the original payment method. Depending on the customer's financial institution, the refund process may take between 7 and 14 working days.

Service Policy

RINELLA LUXE is dedicated to providing professional, reliable, and customer-focused services. The company aims to ensure that every customer receives high-quality products and a pleasant shopping experience.

Customer inquiries submitted through email, social media platforms, or other communication channels will be responded to within 24–48 business hours.
